Tacos

This crunchy tex-mex classic is made with seasoned meat, plenty of cheese, tomatoes, and crisp lettuce. This is a easy to make meal that even the kids can get in on. Just watch out for your helpers snacking on the ingredients!

The meat is made from ground beef, taco seasoning, canned tomatoes, and an onion. The beef is fried in a pan until brown. After draining the fat, add the onions and saut√©ed until clear. Then simply add the seasoning and tomatoes. Continue cooking until the beef absorb the liquids.

While the beef is cooking you can prepare the toppings and shells, with dinner on the table in about a half-hour.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 package of taco spices
  • 1 lbs of ground beef
  • 1 18oz can of tomatoes
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of diced lettuce

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium high heat.
  2. Add the ground beef and break up with a spatula. Drain fat.
  3. Add the onion to the pan.
  4. Cook, stirring occasionally, until beef is done and onion is soft, 5-6 minutes. Drain off any excess fat.
  5. Add the taco seasonings.
  6. Add the tomatoes to the pan and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Spoon the beef into the taco shells and add toppings such as lettuce, tomatoes and cheese. Serve immediately.
